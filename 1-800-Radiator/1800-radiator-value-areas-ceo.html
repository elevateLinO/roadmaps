1-800-Radiator · Feature planning · Driven Brands
From today’s experience to the platform we need:
Value area by value area
Executive summary of current state and target state across seven value areas from the planning workbook.
Seven value areas where the roadmap concentrates impact.
1
Conversion & Acquisition
High impact
Today
The site is effectively invisible to prospective accounts. No pre-login content, no catalog browsing, no SEO footprint. A mechanic searching for a cooling specialist online will not find 1800R.
Target state
A public-facing marketing presence that demonstrates category authority, enables anonymous catalog browsing, and converts new shop visits into account creation — before a single FSR visit.
2
Customer Experience
High impact
Today
The shop experience is identical for every account regardless of tenure, purchase history, or shop type. Mechanics log in to a gray UI with no guidance, no delivery visibility, and no personalization.
Target state
A personalized, frictionless post-login experience with real-time delivery tracking, guided onboarding, rep visibility, and a dashboard that reflects each shop's actual relationship with 1800R.
3
Operational Efficiency
High impact
Today
Order status, delivery ETAs, and account financials require a phone call to find out. FSRs have no digital tools at the point of contact. Marketing is a structural bottleneck serving 200 locations through two people.
Target state
Self-serve delivery tracking, automated delay notifications, digital FSR enrollment, and a franchise marketing portal — eliminating avoidable call volume and unblocking the marketing team.
4
Financial Visibility
High impact
Today
Shops cannot see their own statements, outstanding invoices, or credit line. Over 50% of customers pay COD via cash or check collected by a driver. Payment allocation is manual and invisible to the account.
Target state
Statements, invoices, and credit visibility available in the portal. A digital payment workflow for COD accounts. Self-serve payment allocation replacing a fully manual AR process.
5
Search & Discovery Authority
High impact
Today
1800R has no indexable public content. AI systems cite competitors as the default authority for cooling system questions. The company's 35 years of specialist knowledge — its most valuable differentiator — generates zero digital reach.
Target state
A structured content program, technical knowledge hub, and AI-optimized content strategy that positions 1800R as the cited authority for cooling system expertise across search and AI platforms.
6
Marketing Enablement
Medium impact
Today
All marketing is physical — flyers, coupons, mailers — with no digital equivalent, no measurable engagement, and no follow-through after the FSR visit.
Target state
QR-linked FSR materials, product packaging touchpoints, a digital promotions engine, and a franchise self-serve portal — converting 1 million annual FSR visits into measurable digital engagement.
7
Technical Foundation
High impact
Today
The site is a single-page application sitting directly on legacy stored procedures. No analytics, no API layer, no modern deployment infrastructure. Every new feature requires backend surgery.
Target state
DEWE platform migration, DUWA data integration, and a stored procedure API layer — establishing the enterprise-grade technical foundation that makes every other feature on this roadmap possible.
